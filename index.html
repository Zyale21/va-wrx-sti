VA WRX STI

The VA WRX STI is the fourth-generation Subaru WRX STI, produced from 2015 to 2021. It features the legendary 2.5L EJ25 turbocharged boxer engine putting out 305 horsepower, paired with Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive for exceptional grip in any condition. Built on Subaru's rally heritage, the VA chassis delivers sharp handling and a raw, engaging driving experience. Learn more at Subaru's official site.
